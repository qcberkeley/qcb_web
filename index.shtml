About Us

In 2020, venture capitalists poured over $683 million into the quantum computing industry. Last year, $1.02 billion was invested. To put the acceleration of this industry in perspective, a mere $187 million was invested in 2019. This industry is expanding faster than ever, and we at QCB want to support students on their journey towards its frontier. We're an undergraduate run club that aims to bridge the gap between students, the quantum computing industry, and academia. We connect students with guest speakers, research projects, panel discussions, lab tours, company visits, and we run an introductory DeCal or discusssion most semesters.

This Semester

This Spring we are focused on enriching and engaging the UC Berkeley community. We are putting our all into research projects and educational initiatives to foster a more advanced, and widely accesssible quantum computing network among undergraduates.
