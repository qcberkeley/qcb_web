Education

QCB's education program spans semester DeCals, course archives, articles, tutorials, and an open resource library for students at every level.

Current · Fall 2026

Quantum Algorithms DeCal

CS 198-148 is a two-unit DeCal run by QCB. We cover quantum circuits and complexity, then get into the algorithms — Grover's, Shor's, Hamiltonian simulation, variational methods, and more. No background in quantum required.

Past DeCals

QCB's DeCal offerings have evolved from introductory quantum computing foundations to hardware, full-stack perspectives, industry applications, and algorithms.

Fall 2022

Full-Stack Quantum Computing

Riley Peterlinz, Binhan Hua

A technical DeCal covering quantum mechanics review, linear algebra, states and gates, quantum algorithms, qubit implementations, industry applications, QML, QAOA, VQE, and a final project.

Spring 2022

Practical Applications of Quantum Computing

Victor Li, Emiliia Dyrenkova, Samyak Surti, Andris Huang

A broad, interdisciplinary DeCal built to introduce the quantum computing industry, separate substance from buzz, and examine applications across business functions and technical domains.

Industry Applications

Fall 2019

Introduction to Quantum Computing

Debayan Bandyopadhyay, Glenn LeBlanc, Ethan Yeh

An introductory course in qubits, gates, measurement, quantum information foundations, circuit-model state evolution, core algorithmic subroutines, QFT, Shor's algorithm, Grover's algorithm, and physical realizations.

Fall 2018

Introduction to Quantum Computing

Daniel Lengyel, Dhruv Devulapalli

An early QCB DeCal introducing qubits, quantum mechanics foundations, entanglement, Bell inequalities, teleportation, FFT/QFT, Shor's algorithm, Grover's algorithm, and special topics in quantum hardware and software.
