Why Sponsor QCB?

QCB is an on-campus organization that provides UC Berkeley undergraduates with a supportive community, interdisciplinary education, and career-advancing opportunities in the quantum technology space. Sponsorship helps us keep those opportunities accessible while connecting partners with students, faculty, and researchers across the deep tech ecosystem.

Students

Reach emerging quantum talent

Connect with UC Berkeley students from physics, computer science, math, engineering, and other STEM fields working on frontier research and development.

Events

Join high-signal programming

Participate in panels, speaker events, workshops, info sessions, networking sessions, and student project presentations.

Ecosystem

Build long-term collaboration

Support cross-field projects, undergraduate research participation, and long-term connections between companies and student organizations.
