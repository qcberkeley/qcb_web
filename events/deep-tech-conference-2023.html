April 2023

Deep Tech Conference 2023

A one-day inter-organizational conference hosted by Quantum Computing at Berkeley to bring together student research and industry perspectives across quantum computing, AI, blockchain, and extended reality.

Overview

A Berkeley Deep Tech Gathering

The 2023 Deep Tech Conference created a shared forum for students working at the edges of emerging technology to present projects in an academic conference format.

QCB organized the event with collaborators from Machine Learning at Berkeley, Blockchain at Berkeley, and Extended Reality at Berkeley, with talks spanning theoretical foundations, near-term applications, and future technical challenges.
